RPG NPC Generator instantly creates believable non-player characters for your tabletop role-playing games.

Perfect for Dungeon Masters who need a memorable innkeeper, a mysterious stranger, or a whole town full of townsfolk on the spot.

Features:
- One-tap random NPC generation: name, nickname, race, age, gender, profession, alignment, sexuality, motivation, languages and personality traits.
- Re-roll any single field with a die, or randomize the whole character at once.
- Save the NPCs you like and browse them any time.
- Edit every detail and add a portrait and your own notes.
- Clean Material 3 interface with light and dark themes.
- Available in English and Portuguese.

100% open source. Supported by ads — remove them for a week anytime by watching a short video.
